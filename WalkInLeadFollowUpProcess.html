  1. Purpose

The purpose of this SOP is to establish a structured follow-up process for scheduled drivers to ensure their attendance, minimize no-shows, and streamline the onboarding process. This process includes WhatsApp reminders, scheduled calls, rescheduling options, CRM updates, and final follow-up actions.

  1. Scope

This SOP applies to Scheduler and Tele caller to the follow-up process for drivers scheduled for a walk-in visit to the office. It includes call attempts, WhatsApp reminders, rescheduling, and final status updates based on the driver's response.

  1. Morning WhatsApp Reminder

  1. The telecaller sends a WhatsApp message to all scheduled drivers in the morning.

  1. Scheduler Calls Scheduled Drivers

  1. The scheduler calls all scheduled drivers for the day.
  2. If the call is connected to the pilot, ask and confirm if the driver is visiting the office.

  1. If the driver confirms the visit:

  1. Update the CRM using disposition: Walk-in Time Fixed.
  2. If the driver needs navigation guidance, provide the required directions and resend the direction video.
  3. Upon arrival, ensure the driver meets the onboarding person
  1. If the driver declines the visit:
  1. Update CRM with the reason provided by the driver.
  2. Ensure to do WhatsApp blast (1 time for 1 day) as a last reminder.
  3. The telecaller will attempt to call the driver 2 times per day for 2 days.
  1. If connected: Follow the Interested Follow-Up Process.
  2. If not connected: Proceed to Robo Calling.
  1. If the call is not connected to the pilot
    If the scheduler is unable to connect with the driver:
  1. Ensure Scheduler attempts calling thrice in the same day to the pilot till(morning, afternoon, evening).
  2. If still not connected, update the status in CRM.
  3. Ensure Tele callers Call that lead for 2 times/day for the next 2 days. If connected, follow-up interesting process and if not connected proceed to Robo calling.

  1. Robo Calling & Final Status

Endure to do robo calling for 1time/day for 2 days If the driver is still not connected move the lead to purge process. If call is connected, Based on the response:

  1. If interested, call back and continue follow-up.
  2. If not interested, move to Purge Process.
