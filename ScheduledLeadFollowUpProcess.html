  1. Purpose

This SOP outlines the structured follow-up calling strategy for Scheduled Leads to ensure timely communication, confirmation, and rescheduling if required. By maintaining consistent engagement, this process improves attendance rates, minimizes no-shows, and enhances overall lead conversion.

  1. Scope
                This process applies to:
  1. Telecallers (TCs) handling scheduled leads follow-ups.
  2. CRM Management Team responsible for updating lead status.
  3. Automation Teams managing WhatsApp and Robo-calling processes.

1

Pitch to Be prepared by Prachi Tiwari

New

Process Development

  1. Extraction & Initial Contact

  1. Ensure Telecaller extracts scheduled lead data for next day from CRM and begins follow-ups before End of Day (EOD).
  2. Customer Call & WhatsApp Message:
  1. Ensure to Send a WhatsApp message containing:
  1. List of required documents for the visit.
  2. Document charges and processing fee details.
  3. App download link.
  1. TC calls the customer to remind them of the appointment.

  1. Pilot Confirmation Call

  1. The telecaller calls the pilot to remind them and confirm if they are visiting tomorrow.
  2. Confirmation Scenarios:
  1. If Confirmed, Thank the pilot and update CRM status.
  2. If Not Confirmed, follow the below steps:
  1. Handling Unconfirmed Pilots

  1. Scenario 1: Pilot Requests to Reschedule

  1. Ensure to Reschedule pilot appointment according to pilot’s availability.

  1. Scenario 2: Pilot Not Reachable

  1. If the pilot’s number is not reachable or the call fails to connect, take the following actions:
  1. Retry the next morning (on the day of the appointment).
  2. Make two call attempts in the morning:
  1. If connected,ensure to remind the pilot and confirm if they are visiting tomorrow.
  2. If the pilot asks to reschedule then Update CRM and reschedule.
  1. If still not reachable after two attempts:
  1. Ensure to Trigger Robo Calling for automated reminders to the pilot.
  2. Mark the lead as "Not Interested" in CRM if no response or been not connected.
  1. If the pilot remains unconnected for more than 15 days:
  1. Send a WhatsApp message with a call button for easy re-engagement.

  1. Scenario 3: Pilot Unable to Confirm

  1. If the pilot cannot confirm about his presence at the scheduled time of appointment at that moment, call again the next day to remind and confirm.
  2. If unresponsive after multiple attempts, follow the Robo Calling & WhatsApp messaging process.

  1. Exception Handling

  1. If CRM data is incorrect or outdated, escalate the issue to the CRM Team for verification.
  2. If the lead requests cancellation, update the status immediately to avoid unnecessary follow-ups.
  3. If multiple pilots for the same schedule are unconfirmed, prioritize high-conversion leads for re-engagement.
