  1. Purpose

  1. This SOP ensures that disconnected calls are systematically reattempted to minimize drop-offs and improve customer engagement. Calls that get disconnected within a short duration are handled through the Abandoned Process, ensuring a structured approach for re-calling.

  1. Scope

  1. This process applies to:
  1. Inbound (IB) Calls disconnected within 10 seconds.
  2. Outbound (OB) Calls disconnected within 5 seconds.
  3. Telecallers (TCs) responsible for managing re-calls.
  4. Dialer Management Team ensures proper execution of the ABD process.

  1. Handelling call drops:

  1. Identifying Disconnected Calls

  1. IB Calls disconnected within 10 seconds are flagged for re-call.
  2. OB Calls disconnected within 5 seconds are flagged for re-call.

  1. Handling Disconnected Calls – ABD Process

  1. Automatic Re-Call Attempt:
  1. The system re-attempts the call within a predefined timeframe.
  2. If connected, proceed with the standard pitch and FAQs.
  1. If Call Remains Disconnected:
  1. The lead is flagged as Abandoned.
  2. The ABD Process is followed.
  1. Retry 3 times per day for 48 hours.
  2. If unanswered, move to the PWA Logic (Priority, Weightage, Max Attempts).
  3. If answered, continue with the standard calling process.
